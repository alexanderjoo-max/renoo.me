Why Medical Tourism?

Medical tourism is a rapidly growing industry, with millions of patients traveling abroad each year for affordable, high-quality care. Popular procedures include:

  • Cosmetic Surgery: Botox, rhinoplasty, liposuction, breast augmentation
  • Dental Care: Implants, veneers, full smile makeovers
  • Fertility Treatment: IVF and reproductive services
  • Orthopedic Surgery: Hip and knee replacements
  • Eye Surgery: LASIK and corrective procedures

Patients can often save 50-80% compared to prices in the US, UK, or Australia—while receiving care from internationally trained physicians in JCI-accredited facilities.

Our Data

We compile pricing information from verified clinics, government healthcare databases, and medical tourism providers worldwide. Prices shown are typical ranges and may vary based on:

  • Individual clinic and surgeon reputation
  • Complexity of the procedure
  • Anesthesia type and facility fees
  • Pre- and post-operative care included

Always request detailed quotes from specific clinics for accurate pricing.

Top Medical Tourism Destinations

Some of the world's most popular destinations for medical procedures include:

  • Thailand (Bangkok): World-class hospitals with English-speaking staff, unbeatable value for cosmetic and dental procedures
  • Turkey (Istanbul): Advanced cosmetic surgery facilities, particularly hair transplants and facial procedures
  • Mexico (Mexico City, Tijuana): Convenient for North American patients, excellent dental and cosmetic care
  • South Korea (Seoul): Cutting-edge cosmetic surgery technology, especially facial procedures
  • India (Mumbai, Delhi): Highly trained surgeons, incredible value for complex surgeries
