Safety & Standards

Accreditation to Look For

  • JCI (Joint Commission International) — the gold standard for international hospital accreditation
  • Board-certified practitioners — verify credentials in the country's medical registry
  • Genuine Botulinum Toxin brands — Allergan (Botox), Galderma (Dysport), or Merz (Xeomin)
  • GCR (Global Clinic Rating) — independent quality scoring for medical tourism clinics

Red Flags Checklist

  • Prices that seem too good to be true (well below country average)
  • No verifiable practitioner credentials or clinic registration
  • Unwillingness to disclose the brand of botulinum toxin used
  • No before/after photos or patient reviews available
  • Pressure to commit or pay in full before consultation
  • Clinic does not provide written aftercare instructions
